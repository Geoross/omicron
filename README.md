# OMICRON
**A streamlined YouTube Video & Audio Downloader**
*Developed by George Rossis* ---

## ⚡ WHAT IS OMICRON?
Omicron is a lightweight, powerful desktop utility built to do one thing perfectly: download high-quality video and audio from YouTube. No ads, no web-browser bloat, just a clean interface and fast results. 

## 🛠️ HOW TO USE
Using Omicron is as easy as ordering takeout. Here is how to get your files:

1. **Open the App:** Just double-click the Omicron app to launch it.
2. **Pick Your Pantry:** Look at the bottom of the screen. Click the "Change" buttons to tell the app exactly which folders you want your Videos and Audio saved to. 
3. **Feed It a Link:** Copy the link of the YouTube video you want, and paste it right into the main text box.
4. **Choose Your Quality:** Click the dropdown menu next to the link to pick how you want it saved. You can choose high-quality video (like "Crisp 1080p") or just rip the audio ("Just the Tunes").
5. **Add the Extras (Optional):** * **Playlists:** Downloading a whole playlist? Check the **Playlist 📜** box, and a menu will pop up letting you pick exactly which videos to keep.
   * **Pro Options:** You can also check the boxes to grab subtitles, add album art, or even type in specific timestamps to slice out just a small clip of the video!
6. **Let It Cook:** Click the big **Munch It! 🦖** button (or press Enter on your keyboard). Your download will pop into the queue below, and you can watch the progress bar as it finishes up.

*(Note: Omicron needs to be connected to the internet to grab your videos!)*
